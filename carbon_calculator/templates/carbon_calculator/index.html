Why Monitor CO₂

Planet Earth is our home and would be our first home no matter how far scientific exploration goes, but it seems that we are destroying our home gradually and this is a big problem.

The big question is “WHY SHOULD THE PROBLM BE SOLVED?” There are varieties of reasons why this problem should be solved, which includes but not limited to the fact that if this problem is solved, it would reduce the occurrence of respiratory diseases that arise due to air pollution, it would also raise awareness of importance of electric vehicles in reducing CO₂ emission and more importantly, it would boost Nigeria’s economy whereby Nigerian Innovators can contribute to improvements on electric vehicles and reduce importation of vehicles to barest minimum.

You can use this Web Application to check the level of CO₂ a vehicle would emit. The differences might be little,but it’s the little things that adds up to become big.
